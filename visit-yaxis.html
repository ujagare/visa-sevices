Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about visit visa applications and requirements

What documents are required for a tourist visa application?

Common documents include a valid passport, completed visa application form, passport-sized photographs, proof of accommodation, travel itinerary, travel insurance, bank statements showing financial capability, and a letter explaining the purpose of your visit. Specific requirements may vary by country.

How long does it take to process a visit visa?

Processing times vary by country and visa type. Tourist visas typically take 5-15 business days, while business visas may take 7-20 business days. Some countries offer expedited processing for an additional fee. We provide estimated processing times during your consultation.

Can I extend my stay once I'm in the country?

Extension possibilities depend on the country's immigration policies. Some countries allow visitors to apply for extensions before their current visa expires, while others require you to exit and re-apply. We can advise on specific country regulations and help with extension applications where possible.

What happens if my visa application is rejected?

If your application is rejected, we'll analyze the reasons for rejection and advise on the best course of action. Options may include addressing the specific concerns and reapplying, appealing the decision if applicable, or considering alternative destinations. Our expertise helps minimize rejection risks through proper preparation.
